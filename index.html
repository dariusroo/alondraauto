Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

We happily take walk-ins at any time. Calling ahead is a good idea, especially for larger repairs, but customers can also contact the shop directly to ask about current availability.

What kinds of vehicles do you work on?

We are capable of doing repairs on almost every vehicle on the market. Call to confirm support for your specific make, model, and issue.

How long will my repair take?

Timing depends on the problem, needed parts, and workload. Small maintenance jobs may take a few hours, while larger repairs can take a few days to weeks.
